Warehouse Floor Design & Data Mapping

Visual Layout • Data Integration • Operational Insights

Situation

The warehouse lacked an accurate and visually clear floor plan. Because this layout was not connected to live operational data, it was difficult to quickly detect stock discrepancies, correctly assess capacity, and monitor deviations. Teams operated without a visual reference, limiting operational efficiency.

My Role
  • Designed and structured a scale-accurate warehouse floor plan based on physical measurements
  • Linked physical warehouse locations to digital data (stock levels, volumes, SKUs)
  • Analyzed data flows to identify patterns and operational anomalies
  • Developed a UX concept for an interactive floor management tool
  • Worked closely with operations teams to validate assumptions and optimize outcomes
Approach
  • Physically measured all warehouse zones and translated them into a scale-accurate digital floor plan
  • Created an initial visual concept using wireframes and prototypes (Figma)
  • Validated location codes and XYZ coordinates within the WMS data structure (JDA)
  • Linked location and stock data using Snowflake SQL
  • Developed logic to provide visual insight into:
    • Overstock and understock per zone
    • Available free locations
    • Deviations in volume or SKU distribution
  • Visualized location-based data and insights using Tableau
  • Shared results with leadership to support operational optimization
Impact
  • Faster detection of stock discrepancies and operational deviations
  • Improved capacity planning through location-based visual insights
  • Clear overview for team leaders and managers to enable faster decision-making
  • Established a strong foundation for a data-driven, interactive warehouse management solution
Tools & Skills
  • Tools: Snowflake SQL, Tableau, JDA (WMS), Figma, CAD-based floor mapping tool
  • Skills: Spatial data mapping, data validation, operational analytics, UX concept development, analytical modeling

HR Analytics Dashboard

Context

Our organization needed better insights into key HR metrics such as turnover, absenteeism, and productivity by team or location. Existing reports were built manually in Excel and PowerPoint, leading to errors and outdated data. Additionally, HR and operational data were spread across different systems, making it hard to get a consistent overview.

Develop a scalable and reliable HR dashboard that integrates HR and operational data. The tool should help HR, operations, and management make faster, data-driven decisions around workforce planning, turnover, and employee deployment.

My Role & Team
  • Led the data analysis, engineering, and dashboard development from end to end
  • Collaborated closely with HR and BI teams to align on definitions and KPIs

  • HR: defining KPIs and validating concepts (turnover, contracts, absences)
  • BI & IT: integrating operational systems and accessing WMS data

  • Agile delivery in SCRUM sprints
  • Held workshops with HR and management to gather reporting needs and business questions
  • Structured data from HR and WMS to ensure consistency
  • Engineered a robust dataset in Snowflake SQL with calculated fields for turnover, absenteeism, contract types, site productivity, etc.

  • Validated data with HR experts and Excel-based parallel testing
  • Rolled out in production with documentation and training

  • Real-time visibility into turnover, absences, and productivity by site
  • Improved collaboration between HR and operations via a shared dashboard
  • Supported strategic decisions on workforce planning and retention

Inbound & Customer Service Process Automation

Inbound Operations • Workflow Automation • Sustainability Analytics

Situation

Inbound workflows were largely managed through disconnected Excel files, emails, and temporary tooling. This resulted in inconsistencies, limited traceability, and slow information flow between operations and customer service. In addition, there was no central and reliable data stream to analyze inbound performance, issues, and sustainability metrics.

My Role
  • Initiator and owner of the digitalization of inbound and customer service processes
  • Analysis of existing operational workflows and bottlenecks
  • Implementation of monday.com as a central workflow and communication platform
  • Data integration and modeling for inbound overviews and analysis
  • Active contribution to the development of an internal APEX tool for data capture
  • Execution of sustainability analyses, including container fill rate
  • Close collaboration with operations, planners, customer service, IT, and BI teams
Approach
  • Mapped existing Excel- and email-based processes and translated them into standardized digital workflows
  • Phased implementation approach:
    • Optimization and standardization of Excel registrations
    • Introduction of Trello and later monday.com for operational tracking and collaboration
    • Development of an internal APEX tool for automated capture of inbound events missing from the WMS
  • Integration and modeling of inbound data (shipments, volumes, timings, issues) for reporting and analysis
  • Creation of centralized inbound datasets to support dashboards and management reporting
  • Execution of container fill rate analyses as a benchmark between own freight and customer transport
