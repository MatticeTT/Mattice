Bleckmann relied on multiple disconnected data sources (WMS, TMS, HR/Syntegro, budgets), resulting in the absence of a single, uniform, and reliable reporting layer for both clients and internal stakeholders. Warehouse Floor Design & Data Mapping

Visual Layout • Data Integration • Operational Insights

Situation

The warehouse lacked an accurate and visually clear floor plan. Because this layout was not connected to live operational data, it was difficult to quickly detect stock discrepancies, correctly assess capacity, and monitor deviations. Teams operated without a visual reference, limiting operational efficiency.

My Role
  • Designed and structured a scale-accurate warehouse floor plan based on physical measurements
  • Linked physical warehouse locations to digital data (stock levels, volumes, SKUs)
  • Analyzed data flows to identify patterns and operational anomalies
  • Developed a UX concept for an interactive floor management tool
  • Worked closely with operations teams to validate assumptions and optimize outcomes
Approach
  • Physically measured all warehouse zones and translated them into a scale-accurate digital floor plan
  • Created an initial visual concept using wireframes and prototypes (Figma)
  • Validated location codes and XYZ coordinates within the WMS data structure (JDA)
  • Linked location and stock data using Snowflake SQL
  • Developed logic to provide visual insight into:
    • Overstock and understock per zone
    • Available free locations
    • Deviations in volume or SKU distribution
  • Visualized location-based data and insights using Tableau
  • Shared results with leadership to support operational optimization
Impact
  • Faster detection of stock discrepancies and operational deviations
  • Improved capacity planning through location-based visual insights
  • Clear overview for team leaders and managers to enable faster decision-making
  • Established a strong foundation for a data-driven, interactive warehouse management solution
Tools & Skills
  • Tools: Snowflake SQL, Tableau, JDA (WMS), Figma, CAD-based floor mapping tool
  • Skills: Spatial data mapping, data validation, operational analytics, UX concept development, analytical modeling

  • Active contribution to the development of an internal APEX tool for data capture
  • Execution of sustainability analyses, including container fill rate
  • Close collaboration with operations, planners, customer service, IT, and BI teams
